Karim Patel, Sound Stage Manager

A twenty-year veteran of India's film industry, Karim
made the move from Bollywood to Hollywood in 1995
and was hired last year to run the Sunrise lot for
twenty times the salary he received in Bombay. Karim
started working in the entertainment industry when he
was just four years old, an orphan who was hired to
hold an umbrella over an Indian movie star for a shoot
during the Monsoon season. After two years, he was
promoted to "snack boy", a position that exposed
Karim to the inner workings of a movie studio. He
toiled on over 400 musicals in India before saving up
enough money to come to America.
