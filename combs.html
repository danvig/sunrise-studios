Helen Combs, Story Editor

Helen got her entertainment start when she landed a
job with the New Jersey-based Ultimate Cage Fights in
promotions right out of the University of Alabama.
There she developed an interest in various fighting
techniques and trained in Cra-Ma-Ga. She was the
New Jersey Amateur Martial Arts Champion for women
18-25 in 1992. After two years with UCF, Anita moved
west and worked in sales for Kodak. From there, she
worked in development for Ben Vereen and moved
over to the Sunrise team in 1997. She is a wonderful
asset to the company as she discovered the script for
the upcoming Murder of the Month Club.
