Steve Carter, Executive Story Editor

Steve is a 1985 graduate of NYU's prestigious film
school. After his education, he went to work for indie
producer Flash Corrigan. As the Chief Development
Executive for Corrigan Flicks, Carter discovered
acclaimed director Lionel Washington and introduced
him to Sunrise Studios for the jointly-produced hit
Gimme Some! Steve left Flash in 1992 with nine
produced films under his belt and took a post at
sitcom actor Frank Wilson's company Laugh-A-Lot.
Under a deal at Warner Brothers, Frank and Steve
produced the Little House series. In 1995, Carter set
up a company with up-and-coming writer Will
Kennison and had a big hand in the success of Stab
and Stab 2. In 1998, Don Crosby hand-picked Steve
as his chief story editor. He reports directly to Ms.
Virginia Grey.
