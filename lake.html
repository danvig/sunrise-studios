Roland F. Lake, CFO/Legal

Mr. Lake is a graduate of Princeton University and Yale
Law. After three years in the Dade County, Florida
DA's office, he joined Sunrise in 1977. Lake has
consistently mixed his corporate and prosecution skills
to make the Studio a fun, smoothly-functioning and
safe environment for all its employees. In addition to
his numerous duties in the legal department for
Sunrise, Mr. Lake has earned several producer credits
on Studio films, of which is he is extremely proud.
Roland Lake was a consultant on several highly
publicized defense cases in Los Angeles county for a
number of top profile clients, including members of
O.J. Simpson's "dream team". Mr. Lake has received
numerous awards over the years, including
"Entertainment Attorney of the Year" in 1983. He has
a loving wife, Caron, and six children, Vanessa, Roland
Jr, Damon, Kevin, Candice, and Rose.
