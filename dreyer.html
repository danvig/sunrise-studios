Anita Dreyer, Director, Creative & Media

A native of Toronto, Canada, Anita majored in new
technology at the University of Vermont. After a
misunderstanding with the FBI over a hacking charge
was dismissed, Anita came to Sunrise to spearhead
the Studio's arrival on the information superhighway.
Dubbed "the hardest working woman in the southwest
sector of the lot," Anita is virtually unrecognizable
without her laptop and telephone headset. As she is
quick to remind us, it was her ambition and
perseverance that put the new Sunrise site in motion.
Congratulations, Anita, on being named Crosby's "VIP
of the month"!
