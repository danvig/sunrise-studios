Dave Greenfield, Transportation

A graduate of Columbia University's prestigious film
program, Dave Greenfield won a Student Academy
Award in 1990 for his thesis project, Sleigh Ride.
Acclaim for the movie landed Dave an agent at CAA
and a deal at Columbia Pictures, where he developed
several projects. Although none made it to the silver
screen, Dave's obvious talents behind-the-wheel at
UPS (as well as his keen creative intellect) caught the
attention of Sunrise's own Don Crosby, who hired the
young man to head Sunrise's transportation
department. Dave was recently married to Sarah
Blum, his therapist. Congratulations, Dave!
