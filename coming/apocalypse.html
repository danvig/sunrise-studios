Apocalypse 2000

Dazzling special effects take center stage in this
harrowing vision of the millenium. The future of the
human race hangs in the balance as our fears of
disaster come true. The only person who can save us is
an old homeless man claiming to be Nostradamus. If
only someone would listen...
