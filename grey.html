Virginia Grey, Creative Executive

A native of New Orleans, Virginia majored in occult
studies at Tulane University and worked part-time as a
fourth-grade teacher. In 1972, Virginia moved to Los
Angeles with her boyfriend, actor Paul Drake (Who
Said That?,) and landed an internship at the newly
formed studio. She has been with the Studio for over
twenty-seven years, working in such capacities as
Story Editor, Associate Producer (Frankenpimp, The
Butcher, The Baker, The Candlestick Killer, Dr. Jugular), Executive Story Editor,
Development Executive, Junior Director of
Development, Director of Development, and Creative Executive. No trip to
Sunrise is complete without a visit to Virginia's office
and a close inspection of her wide collection of voodoo
dolls.
