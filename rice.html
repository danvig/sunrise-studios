Heather Rice, Senior V.P., Acquisitions & Distribution

Nobody has more frequent flier miles than Heather
Rice, our executive responsible for buying the latest
films and ensuring Sunrise projects are shown in the
best venues. Heather got her start at Drive-In
Entertainment, a small distribution company in the
Midwest in 1982 before being introduced to Don
Crosby at ShoWest in 1984. By 1985, Heather was
working for Sunrise, searching the globe for pictures
like We're Gonna Take a Ride and Goin' Postal. In fact,
it was Heather's eye for talent that discovered young
black director Lionel Washington and his comedy
Gimme Some! at the Song & Dance Film Festival in
Boise. Heather continues to be the company's #1
schmoozer and we look forward to many more years
with her.
