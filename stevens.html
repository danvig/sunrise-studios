Martin Stevens, Commissary

The long line every lunchtime at the Commissary is
the fault of the talented Martin Stevens, the Studio
Chef responsible for such delicious favorites as Victor
Raven's Chinese Chicken Salad, Dr. Jugular's Braised
Beef Brisket Sandwich, and the Tori Spelling
Southwestern Baked Potato Bar. Trained as a chef at
the Culinary Institute in Hyde Park, New York, Martin
started a family while working at the Redeye Grill in
Cleveland. After moving up to stints as Les Chez and
the Heights Bistro, Martin was invited to study in LA
with such greats as Wolfgang Puck. He has been
treating Sunrise employees to his delicious creations
for the past fifteen years.
