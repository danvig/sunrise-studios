Don Gordon, Chairman in Memorium

As Mr. Crosby mentioned at his best friend's eulogy in
1996, "The Sun' would never have risen without Don
Gordon." Indeed, every film ever created at the Studio
bore the marks of Don Gordon's distinct personality:
bold, courageous, and groundbreaking. Sunrise
Studios was Don's passion and the Studio was built
from his creative energy and enthusiasm. The result is
a library of entertaining and original films that have
entertained millions over the last three decades. As
Mr. Crosby eloquently put it, "Pioneers are rare, like
jewels. Today, the world has lost a diamond."
