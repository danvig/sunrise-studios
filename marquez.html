Roberta Marquez, Administration

Roberta enjoyed an education in Art History at Harbor
Community College and UCLA. She went on to earn an
MFA from Boston's School of Fine Arts in 1989 as an
acclaimed sculptor. After five years in Aspen, Colorado,
where she received tremendous commercial success,
great media response, and several retrospectives
around the globe, Roberta was forced to retire from
the art world due to a freak accident involving a
kitchen knife. Never one to be discouraged by the
hand dealt her, Marquez moved to Los Angeles and ran
the administration of the small private arts & sciences
school, Crossroads. In 1997, she took a job under
former Sunrise Administration head, Claire Redbound.
When our old friend retired, Roberta took over and has
made sure that the studio has been running smoothly
ever since.
