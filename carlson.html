Cody Carlson, Intern of the Month

A senior in NYU's cinema studies department and a
Queens native, Cody comes to Sunrise as part of our
Little Suns program, designed to give college students
on-the-job experience before graduation. In exchange
for his diligent hardwork, Cody is finishing his thesis
film, Locomotion in the Sunrise editing suite. Carlson
is also a Stab fanatic, and nobody on the lot (with the
exception of Will Kennison!) knows more about the
series.
