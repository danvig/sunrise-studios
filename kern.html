James Kern, Studio Operations

At twenty-nine, James is one of the youngest people
ever to run the day-to-day operations of a major
studio. He comes to us by-way of Middlebury College,
where he graduated Magna-Cum-Laude and starred on
the school's nationally ranked ultimate frisbee team.
After a year racing off-road vehicles, he turned to the
film industry. James produced over thirty music videos
and industrials for the LA based Urbanos Milagros
Productions. In 1996, he took a job with Dick Clark
Productions and was soon at the helm of the day-to-
day production. In 1998, he accepted an offer to join
the Sunrise family in his current capacity. James still
keeps his hand in the music business as he has just
Executive Produced an album by urban artist Shoshoni
entitled Ghost Dance. Look for it at your local record
store!
